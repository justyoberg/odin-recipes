Macaroni and Cheese recipe

This recipe is a family favorite in my home. It is one of the most delectable Macaroni and Cheese dishes I have ever eaten. It is fantastic, and yes I need to drag this out to make it an actual paragraph.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 3 (8 ounce) containers cottage cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a large bowl combine cooked pasta, cottage cheese, sour cream, eggs, Cheddar cheese, and cayenne pepper. Mix well and transfer to prepared dish. Sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Cover loosely with aluminum foil and bake 40 minutes.
